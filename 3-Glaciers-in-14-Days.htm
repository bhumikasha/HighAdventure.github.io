3 Glaciers in 14 Days :
Pindari, Kafni & Sundardunga Glacier

Trek Region: Uttarakhand (Kumaun) Himalayas INDIA

SPECIAL FEATURES: Village, Glaciers, High peaks

Base: LOHARKHET Total Duration: 14 Days

Trekking days: 12 Trek grade: Moderate One way trek :77 Kms

Max altitude : 3800 m. Best time for trekking : May June, September, October, November

Trek Itinerary :

Trekking tour day 1:- Welcome in Kathgodam railway station then drive to Kausani Check in Hotel/resort.

Trekking tour day 2:- Drive to Song / Loharkhet then trek to Talla Dhakuri High adventure camp.

Trekking tour day 3:- Trek to Khati village. Over night camping.

Trekking tour day 4:- Trek to Khatia. Over night camping.

Trekking tour day 5:- After breakfast trek to Kafni glacier. You can touch to the glacier and enjoy the mountain views back to Dwali camp.

Trekking tour day 6:- After breakfast trek to Pindari Glacier. Over night camping.

Trekking tour day 7:- After enjoying the sun rise view of the o pint we will trek to Dwali camp.

Trekking tour day 8:- Trek to khati village and after lunch way down to Bacham then Jatoli village for night camping /or home stay.

Trekking tour day 9:- Trek to Katlaya for night camp.

Trekking tour day 10:- Visit sundardunga and Baluni top and back to camp side.

Trekking tour day 11:- After breakfast trek to Jatoli Night stay.

Trekking tour day 12:- Trek to Dhakuri camp.

Trekking tour day 13:- Trek to Loharkhet then drive to Kausani.

Trekking tour day 14:- After lunch drive to Kathgodam railway station.

Tour end
