Adi Kailash & Om Parvat

Adi Kailash (4770 M) also known as Chhota Kailash is an area of great natural beauty, peace and serenity. It lies on the main route that is followed for kailash manassarovar yatra. Adi Kailash is an ancient holy place in the Himalayan Range, close to the Indo-Tibetan border. This journey takes the mind and soul into the heartland of awe-inspiring natural beauty and splendor.

Trek Region: Uttarakhand (Kumaun) Himalayas INDIA

Total Duration: 17Days

Trekking days: 13

Trek grade: challenging One way trek : Kms

Max altitude : 3800 m

Best time for trekking : 15th to 15th November ( Expect July and August )

Recommendations:- Previous walking experience is helpful. We do not recommend this tour to people with chronic problems like epilepsy and those related to blood pressure or heart.


Accessibility : Ranikhet Exp. N.5013 (Daily) Departure at old Delhi Railway station 22.45 / Tourist bus Delhi to Kathgodam


Route of the trail ( Itinerary)

Trekking Tour

Day 01:- Morning arrival at Kathgodam then drive to Chaukori .
Check in at hotel.

Day 02:- After breakfast drive to Dharchula.

Day 03:- Drive to Mangti then trek to Gala.

Day 04:- Trek to Bundi

Day 05:- Trek to Gungi

Day 06:- Trek to Navidhang for Om parvat.

Day 07:- Trek to Kalapani (Kali Tample)

Day 08:- Trek to Gungi

Day 09:- Trek to Kutti

Day 10:- Trek to Jolingkong for Adi Kailash

Day11:- Adi Kailash darshan Visit Parvati Sarovar.

Day 12:- Trek to Kutti

Day 13:- Trek to Gungi

Day 14:- Trek to Bundi.

Day 15:- Trek to Mangati then drive to Dharchula. Check in at hotel.

Day 16:- Drive to Chukori. Check in at hotel.
