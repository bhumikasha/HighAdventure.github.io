Kedarnath Pawalikantha

The high altitude meadows of Garhwal Himalayas, apart form its thrilling landscape is specially Nature has best endowed Garhwal with magnificent natural resources. Panwali Kantha, one of known for the spectacular display of the flowering herbs. At an altitude of 3658 meters, this meadow lies in the northern part of Garhwal Himalayas. One can also encounter high altitude animals e.g Bharal, Ghoral, Himalayan Bear, and the rate musk deer.

Trek Region:-Uttarakhand (Garhwal) Himalayas INDIA

One Way Trek:- 69 Kms.

Total duration:- 07 Days

Trekking days:- 05 Days

Trek grade:- Moderate to strenuous

Max altitude:- 3970 m

Best time for trekking:-May to October

Accessibility : Take the overnight Mussoorie Express (10:15pm) from Delhi to reach Haridwar early in the morning.

Route of the trail ( Itinerary):-

Day 01:- Arrival in the morning at Haridwar then drive to drive to Ghuttu 1524m. It is a small Garhwali village and starting point of the trek, Lunch on the way. Overnight home stay or camp.

Day 02:- After breakfast we start trek for Panwali 3970 m. The total trek is 18 Km. Arrive and camp in the meadow near a stream, as the entire meadow is full of crystal clear springs. Night stay on tent.

Day 03:- After breakfast 15 km. trek to Magguchatti 3086m. The entire trek is on rocky terrain and is strenuous. Arrive in Magguchatti camp. Night stay on tent.

Day 04:- After breakfast 8 km. trek to Trijugi Narayan 1982m., a legendry village where Shiva & Parvati were wedded. Arrive and visit the temple. After exploring the village we drive to Gaurikund. Check in Hotel.

Day 05:- After breakfast visit the Gaurikund temple and the hot springs, then 14 km. trek to Kedarnath. The entire trek is gradual ascent till we reach Kedarnath. Check in the Guest House.

Day 06:- Morning visit Kedarnath temple 3580m.and after breakfast, explore the holy river Mandakini then 14 km. trek to Gorikund. Check in Hotel. Enjoy hot water bath in Gorikund.

Day 07:- After breakfast drive to Haridwar railway station.

Tour End
