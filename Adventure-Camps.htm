Adventure camps

Mountain Biking in Nainital ( Kumaun Himalayas ) Uttarakhand

Day 1:- Pickup from Kathgodam railway station, drive to Nainital. Check in Fair light trail Resort. After break fast visit Snow view (250 km. range of Himalaya) then trek down or by Ropeway (aerial express) to mall road. After lunch enjoy walking to Thandi Sadak and Explore mall road then back to the Fair light trails Resort for Night halt.

Day 2:- After breakfast enjoy mountain biking to Vinayak camp (12 k.m.) The road goes along the foot hills and passes through oak and deodar forests with a huge variety of plants facing the high Himalaya and abounding in wild life and bird life.
Check in the camp vinayak.

Day 3:- After breakfast enjoy mountain biking to Kungkharak camp (15 k.m.), Enjoy the natural beauty is reassuring and rejuvenating offering Rolling Meadows, Jagged rock faces, scenic valley with hospitable people having rich culture and fascinating tradition.
Check in the camp Kunjkharak.

Day 4:- After breakfast drive to Nainital. Check in fair light Fair light trails resort for night stay.

Day 5:- Morning after breakfast day free for local visit or personal shopping, in the evening drive to Kathgodam Railway station.

Tour end


Adventure camping in Binsar

Enjoy flora and fauna, Bird watching, Mountain Biking, night trek, camp fire and Dinner. Pure camping in Real Camp Binsar (Wild Life Sanctuary) Enjoy splendid Himalayan view of Chaukhamba (7138m.), Trishul(7120m.), Nanda devi(7816m.), Nanda kot(6861m.),Nanda ghunti(6020m.) And Panchchuli peaks (6904m.) etc.

Approach: - Delhi to Kathgodam (5013 Ranikhet Exp.)
Kathgodam
Kathgodam to Real Camp Binsar 3 ½ hrs.

Plan 1:- (Programme for those who want a new experience )

Participation - Age Group:- All age ( medically fit )

Itinerary:-

Day 1:- welcome in Kathgodam railway station in the morning then drive to camp at 10 a.m. welcome drink.After lunch walk to village and enjoy cultural life Of Kumauni people, then back to camp, enjoy camp fire and dinner.

Day 2:- After breakfast drive to Binsar Sanctuary for birding and Himalayan view, lunch on the way. Back to camp, Enjoy camp fire and dinner.

Day 3:-After breakfast enjoy mountain biking/ sort trekking, lunch on the way. Back to camp, in the evening visit to local village and then back for dinner.

Day 4:- After breakfast drive to Nainital, day free for shopping and other personal work.

Day5:- Full day free for local visit, evening drive to Delhi.

Tour End

Plan 2:- (programme for bird watchers, and Nature lovers)

Participation - Age Group:- 15 to 50 Years

Itinerary:-

Day1:- Pick up from Kathgodam railway station. Arrival in the camp, after lunch briefing on camp rules etc.

Day2:- After breakfast trekking to Binsar, searching the flora (Herbal identification) and enjoy the view of great Himalayas. lunch on the way, back to camp.

Day3:- After breakfast Bird watching trip.
Back for lunch then village walk (know the culture of Kumauni people) back to camp.

Day4:- After breakfast enjoy mountain biking, lunch on the way. Back to camp, Enjoy Camp fire and dinner.

Day5:- After breakfast drive to Naintal, Check in Hotel for rest and relax. Evening enjoy the Mall.

Day6:- Full day free for local visit, evening drive to Delhi.

Tour End

Plan 3:- (Only For Mountain bikers)

Participation - Age Group:- 15 to 40 Years

Itinerary:-

Day1:- Pick up from Kathgodam railway station. Arrival in camp. In the evening, briefing on camp rules and mountain biking.

Day2:- After breakfast, mountain biking to N T D then back to camp via Kasardevi.

Day3:- After Breakfast Mountain biking to Binsar 0 point then back to camp.

Day4:- After breakfast Mountain biking to Dholchina and back to Camp.

Day5:- After breakfast drive to Naintal, Check in Hotel for rest and relax. Evening enjoy the Mall.

Day6:- Full day free for local visit, evening drive to Delhi.

Tour End
