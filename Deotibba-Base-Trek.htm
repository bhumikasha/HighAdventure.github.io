DEO TIBBA GLACIER (Adventure Trek)

Trek Region:- Himachal (MANALI) INDIA

One Way Trek:- 64 Kms.

Total duration:- 09 Days

Trekking days:- 09 Days

Trek grade:- Moderate

Max altitude:-

Accessibility: Drive by Volvo bus from Delhi at 05:00 P.M.

Route of the trail (Itinerary):-

Day 01 :- Morning arrival at Manali at 9:30 A.M. Check in Hotel. Evening visit to the Manali market.

Day 02:-Leave Manali early in the morning and trek up to the next Camp Tilgan. Night camping.

Day 03:- After breakfast trek to Sarra bogi camp.

Day 04:- Trek to Kharimindiyari .

Day 05:- After breakfast Trek to Zameer (3300m.) 5 hours. Today’s trek is an easy one with lots of streams, juniper and open space.

Day 06:- Trek to Piyangniru. One can have a very close view of the mountain peaks, glacier valleys, hanging glacier, crevasses and a heart touching scenic panoramic view of almighty Deo Tibba peak. After having lunch visit to Deo Tibba glacier for look in to the fetching view of the Deo Tibba snout, which originates Jhobri River (3800m.) and back to camp.

Day 07:- This trek has tackle tricky snow covered slopes in a zigzag way and steep portion to be climbed right up to top of the summit hill (3880m.) and after that gradually all the way descend down to Raurikhauri snow camp (3850m). At Raurikhauri one can have a good scenic beauty of a small frozen mountain tarn on the treeless landscape and snow capped mountains view of both Dhauladhar and Pirpanjal ranges.

Day 08:- After breakfast Trek descends along the Lama dug pastures and through dense forest right up to Hamta village, then way down to Manali road head. Stay overnight at Manali hotel.

Day 09:- Half day free and in the evening, departure by bus to Delhi.

Tour End
